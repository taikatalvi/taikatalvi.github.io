Game Description

A classic Ping Pong game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript, reimagined as a neon arcade cabinet. Control your paddle to play against the AI and try to score more points!

Controls

  • Mouse: Move your paddle left and right
  • Touch: Drag on mobile devices
  • Keyboard: Hold arrow keys or A/D to move the paddle
  • Click / tap: Start and pause the game

Features

  • Neon arcade look: synthwave grid, glow and a ball trail
  • Bounce angle depends on where the ball hits the paddle
  • The ball speeds up with every paddle hit
  • AI opponent with human-like reaction
  • Touch, mouse and keyboard support — first to 5 wins
